① RE-READ · 10 MIN · OBSIDIAN OPEN

Re-read your seed.

You will

  • Open Obsidian → Project/seed.md (your W1 note).
  • Read your own words from three weeks ago. Slowly.

② CARVE · 15 MIN · CONSTRAINT IS THE TOOL

Carve v0.1.

v0.1 is not "small for now, big later." v0.1 is the smallest, ugliest version that ships.

Three boxes

  • WHO — one specific person, not "users".
  • WHAT — one verb, one outcome.
  • HOW SOMEONE TESTS IT — under 5 min.

Trap to avoid

"And also it could…" — every "also" makes v0.1 too big. Cut it.

③ BUILD · WATCH-FORS

Two patterns to flag.

Reading without shipping

  • 25 min of docs, 0 min of building = avoidance.
  • Time-box: 5 more min of reading, then put something on screen.
  • Reading is hiding from shipping.

Scope creep

  • "v0.1 has to log in, then save, then…"
  • Interrupt: "What if v0.1 just had the static page?"
  • Almost always: yes. The carve IS the lesson.

④ FIRST PUBLIC TEST · 10 MIN · TWO STICKIES EACH

First public test.

You will

  • Pair up. Show your v0.1 to one classmate.
  • They give ONE observation (yellow sticky) + ONE question (pink sticky).
  • Write both into Bugs/, with the partner's name on each.
  • Switch. Repeat with a different partner.
