DELIVERABLE 1 · v1 DEMO

v1 demo.

40 PTS40
PURPOSE

Why this exists

v1 closes the seed → v0.1 → v1 trajectory we started in W1. Three live demo rounds (pairs · table · open studio) stress-test the artifact across audience sizes and force the student to articulate the trajectory aloud, in real time, three different ways.
TASK

What to do

Demo your v1 to (a) one classmate, (b) a table of four, (c) anyone who walks up in open studio. Each demo narrates the trajectory: seed → v0.1 → v1. Show the artifact, not slides.
CRITERIA

How you're scored

Four criteria, scored Exemplary / Proficient / Developing / Not yet. Total 40 pts. The artifact's polish is one criterion of four — articulation and trajectory matter equally.

DELIVERABLE 2 · 5 BIG IDEAS RECKONING

The reckoning.

40 PTS40
PURPOSE

Why this exists

The 5 Big Ideas are the spine of the course. The reckoning is where they survive past December — or don't. Five lines, one per Big Idea, each referencing your own vault. The exercise forces you to name what stuck and where it lives.
TASK

What to do

In Reflections/W08-reckoning.md, write five lines, one per Big Idea (#1–#5). Each line MUST reference at least one vault note by name. Inside Big Idea #5, re-record the W6 trust dial with one sentence on movement.
CRITERIA

How you're scored

Three criteria, total 40 pts. Specificity is load-bearing — vague reckonings ("this class was great") score below specific ones, even from students whose demos went well.

Rubric — three criteria · four levels

Criterion Exemplary Proficient Developing Not yet PTS

DELIVERABLE 3 · TRAJECTORY + EXPORT

The trajectory.

20 PTS20
PURPOSE

Why this exists

The course ends. The vault doesn't. The trajectory is what makes the externalization habit outlast the syllabus. Naming the next thing you'll ship, and the habit that gets you there, makes both more likely to actually happen.
TASK

What to do

One paragraph in Reflections/W08-trajectory.md: the next thing you're going to ship + the habit from this course that gets you there. Then run "Export your work (.txt)" on any built lesson page and drop the .txt into Canvas as the final artifact.
CRITERIA

How you're scored

Two criteria, total 20 pts. The trajectory is graded on actionability, not ambition.

Rubric — two criteria · four levels

Criterion Exemplary Proficient Developing Not yet PTS
Trajectory paragraph15 pts 15 / 15Names a specific next-thing-to-ship + a specific habit + a specific time-frame ("by Spring break"). All three concrete. 12 / 15Names two of three specifics. 8 / 15Vague aspirations ("keep building", "use my notes more"). 0 / 15Missing or generic Yelp-review. / 15
Final export to Canvas5 pts 5 / 5.txt exported and dropped into Canvas before Sun deadline. 4 / 5.txt exported, late by < 24 hrs. 3 / 5.txt exported, late by > 24 hrs. 0 / 5No export. / 5
