③ AUDIT · 15 MIN · DELIVERABLE #1 · 30 PTS

Paint your 168.

  1. Pick a category per cell
    Sleep · class · study · work · social · screens · transit · other.
  2. Be honest about screens
    Phone screen-time settings have your number. Pull it up. Use it.
  3. Total each category
    The arithmetic is the lesson. Don't argue with the bars.
  4. Mark one leak
    One category you'd take back. That's where Sunday's re-budget starts.

⑥ RE-BUDGET · 12 MIN · DELIVERABLE #3 · 25 PTS

Tomorrow's ABC list.

A
Today, non-negotiable. Max 3.
Each with a realistic time estimate.
Total > 4 hrs? Cut.
Total < 1 hr? Add.
B
Today if A is done.
Important but not on fire.
Most B's quietly become A's by Friday.
That's why MAP exists.
C
Nice if there's air.
Errands · vibes · mild ambition.
Most days, C goes untouched.
That's fine.

⑦ TAKE-HOME · WEEKLY · 50 PTS

The Sunday Ritual.

  1. Pick a Sunday slot
    Same time, same place, every week. 15 minutes.
  2. Run the four-step Loop
    AUDIT (3) · MAP (3) · TRIAGE (5) · RE-BUDGET (4). Same notebook page each week.
  3. Log the gap
    Each weekday: A's planned vs A's done. The gap is the signal.
  4. Bring it to class
    Mondays. We read patterns together for the first 10 minutes.
