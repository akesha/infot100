② ROUND 1 · 15 MIN · LOWEST STAKES

Round 1 — pairs.

You will

  • Pair up. 5 min each direction.
  • Narrate the trajectory: seed (W1) → v0.1 (W4) → v1 (today).
  • Partner asks ONE question, gives ONE observation.

Open the actual code, the actual prototype, the actual document. Even if it's broken. The broken state is still the artifact.

③ ROUND 2 · 20 MIN · TABLE OF 4

Round 2 — table.

You will

  • Form tables of 4.
  • 3 min each: 2 min demo, 1 min Q&A.
  • Hard-stop signal at 3 min. The signal is the boundary.
  • Two observations from the table go into your Bugs/.

Why hard stops

Without the signal, every round leaks. Six minutes per table × five tables = round dies. The signal IS the round.
